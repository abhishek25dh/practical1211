Comparison verdict

      
Best design: the pasted architecture is directionally right, but the best buildable version is narrower: WearAtlas v0 = persistent BodyAtlas + simple PatternGraph + WearState patch engine + deterministic 2.5D renderer. It keeps the “new runtime” idea while avoiding the research trap of full pattern recovery, full 3D, or diffusion-quality rendering on day one.
